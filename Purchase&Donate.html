About us

We are here to share and give the community a full understanding about the sea turtles in the world and mainly focussing on the sri lankan sea turtles who are really in need. Sea turtles have been around for millions of years. Experts predict all of the different species may go extint unless successful sea turtle conservation programs are established and maintained.So lets spread this message and overcome the challenge. Contribute as much as possible to save the sea turtles.
